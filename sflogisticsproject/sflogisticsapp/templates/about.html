About Us

Ocean Frieght

With our broad product range we cover different equipment types and consolidation services to ensure your cargo reaches the right place, at the right time in a cost-efficient way. In order to deliver highest reliability we have planned space protection from every major container port in the world.

  • We have logistics experts specializing in major industry sectors, so we can help you improve your performance and drive out costs.
  • We’re on the ground in over 190 countries allowing you to export and import from more locations worldwide. Providing more ocean loops and services than anyone else.
  • DHL Ocean Freight shipments include a wide range of quality controlled equipment types. We strive for on time deliverability and provide end-to-end visibility.

Air Frieght

Working with carefully selected carriers, we operate with schedules on all the world’s major routes so you can plan with certainty and become more efficient. We offer a range of highly flexible products which allow a choice of delivery speeds to best suit your requirements.

  • Door-to-door service with other options available
  • Defined lead times
  • Optional Cargo Insurance

Land Frieght

Our Road Freight Products offer high quality road transportation, from standard services such as LTL (Less-than-Truck Load), PTL (Part) or FTL (Full-Truck Load) shipments to temperature controlled and highly secured transport

  • International & Domestic Shipments
  • Optional Cargo Insurance
  • Defined lead times
  • Optional Customs Brokerage
  • End-to-end visibility
